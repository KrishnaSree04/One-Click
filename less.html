A Healthy Weight Gain Plan

Here ia a Healthy weight gain diet plan.Follow this for a week see the changes and then continue.

Some Important Weight Gain Foods.

Paneer

Banana

Eggs

Rice

Diet for Monday

Break Fast 2 vegetable stuffed parathas + 1 cup curd and handful of nuts.
Mid Mornning 1 glass lassi or mango shake.
Lunch 1 cup dal/ chicken curry + 1 cup seasonal vegetable + 2 chapattis + ½ cup rice + 1 cup salad.
Evening Snack 1 glass fresh fruit juice + 2 whole-grain toasts.
Dinner 1 cup vegetable salad+ 2 chapattis+ 1 cup salad.

Diet for Tuesday

Break Fast 2 moong dal chilla (paneer stuffing optional), 1 cup curd, handful of nuts.
Mid Mornning Fruit smoothie of any choice.
Lunch 1 cup dal/ chicken curry + 1 cup seasonal vegetable + 2 chapattis + ½ cup rice + 1 cup salad.
Evening Snack 1 bowl tomato soup, 1 cup aloo chat.
Dinner 1 cup vegetable salad+ 2 chapattis+ 1 cup salad.

Diet for Wednesday

Break Fast 1 cup bread upma + 1 cup milk + handful of nuts.
Mid Mornning 2 bananas.
Lunch 1 cup lentils + 1 cup vegetables + 2 chapattis + 1 cup salad.
Evening Snack 1 cup upma + 1 glass vegetable juice.
Dinner 1 cup vegetable + 2 chapattis + 1 cup salad.

Diet for Thursday
